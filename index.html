Intermodal Drayage

Intermodal transportation seamlessly integrates multiple modes of transport like trucks, trains, ships, and planes for efficient, cost-effective, and environmentally friendly freight movement.

01

Dry Van Truck Load

Dry van truckload shipping involves the transportation of freight using standard, enclosed trailers known as dry vans. These trailers are typically used to transport non-perishable goods.

02

Flatbed

Flatbed transportation involves the use of trailers with a flat, open platform for transporting goods. Unlike dry vans, flatbed trailers lack sides and roofs, providing versatility for loading and unloading oversized or irregularly shaped cargo.
